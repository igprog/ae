Welcome to Brela

Brela, the most beautiful place of Makarska Riviera and one of the most beautiful places on the Adriatic coast, the pearl of Croatian tourism is the perfect place for a quiet family holiday. Crystal clear sea, numerous sandy and gravel beaches and walking trails along the coast up to the very tops of the nearby Biokovo mountain will make your vacation an unforgettable experience.

Apartments Elvira

Apartments Elvira are located in Jardula, a quiet part of Brela, located 1500 m from the center. The house is surrounded by pine trees and Mediterranean plants, and is just 50 meters from the beautiful gravel beach. Elvira Apartments are an ideal place to escape from the hustle and bustle. The house has 3 large apartments for a total of 13 people. All apartments have sea views. Parking spaces are provided near the house.
